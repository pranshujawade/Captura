## Join Us as a Brand Partner

### Turn Passion into Purpose

For those who believe in wellness and its power to transform lives, Captura offers more than a partnership — it offers a purpose-led opportunity.

As a Captura Brand Partner, you can share products you truly believe in, build your own wellness community, and earn based on the impact you create.

### Your Benefits

#### Build Your Wellness Community
Introduce others to Captura and help them begin their journey toward conscious, preventive wellness.

#### Earn CapCoins
Receive CapCoins on every eligible product or service purchased by members developed under your community.

#### Convert CapCoins into Earnings
Redeem your CapCoins into your local currency and receive your earnings weekly.

#### Start Your Partner Journey
Purchase Captura products at MRP and begin your journey as a Brand Partner.
